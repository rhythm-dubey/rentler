Deleting Your Account: A Step-by-Step Guide

Deleting your account is a permanent action that cannot be undone. Before proceeding, please ensure you have cancelled any active subscriptions or services linked to your account.

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account using your credentials.
  2. Open the Account / Profile menu.
  3. Choose the “Delete Account” option on the Account page.
  4. Confirm your decision by clicking “Confirm”.
